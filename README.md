# 👟📊 Sneakers & Streetwear Sales Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview

The **Sneakers & Streetwear Sales Dashboard** is an interactive **Power BI Business Intelligence Dashboard** designed to analyze sales performance across brands, product categories, countries, genders, and payment methods.

This dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor key business metrics, identify top-performing products, understand customer purchasing behavior, and make data-driven decisions to improve revenue and profitability.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* ✅ Track Overall Sales Performance
* ✅ Analyze Product & Brand Performance
* ✅ Identify Best-Selling Products
* ✅ Monitor Monthly Revenue Trends
* ✅ Compare Country-Wise Sales
* ✅ Understand Customer Demographics
* ✅ Evaluate Payment Method Preferences
* ✅ Support Data-Driven Business Decisions

## 📈 Business Insights

* 🏆 Nike emerged as the highest revenue-generating brand.
* 👟 Sneakers recorded the highest sales volume across all product categories.
* 📈 July delivered the strongest monthly sales performance.
* 🌍 Japan and Canada contributed the largest share of revenue.
* 👩 Female customers generated the highest revenue contribution.
* 💳 Digital payment methods dominated customer transactions.
* 📦 Sneakers and Hoodies accounted for the majority of product demand.
* 🚀 Mid-year sales growth highlights strong seasonal purchasing trends.
